Landels Tutoring Program

We are a tutoring program that helps first-fifth graders with their homework.

About the Program

We would like to help students with their homework after school on Fridays, and have created a group of four 8th grade students from Crittenden Middle School, to help students with their homework. We would be able to work with students from grades 1-5, we would divide the grades between the team.
